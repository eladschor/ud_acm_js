October 3. 24 hours. Hack. Create. Win.

Innovate

hackD is Delaware's premier student-run hackathon. You'll have 24 hours to develop a product from start to finish.

Have an idea for the next big app? Want to meet and work with other CS majors? Join and we'll pair you with a developer from a technology company who can help guide you through the process. And don't worry, we'll have plenty of food and drinks to keep you going through the night.

Learn and Discover New Technology

Got an idea but don't know how to make it reality? No problem! We'll be running workshops in the weeks leading up to the hackathon, and have a few crash courses during the hackathon. To give you an idea of a few of the topics we'll be covering:

  • Android Apps
  • Web Applications
  • Game Development
