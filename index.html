Our Mission.


The Association for Computing Machinery--ACM for short--is the worldâ€™s largest educational and scientific computing society. Here at UD, we have formed a student chapter for students who deal with computing technologies in their field and for those who are simply interested in the computing world. We hold weekly meetings, host technical workshops, and run hack-a-thons throughout the semetser.

We also seek to help our members understand what life outside of university is like. Some of our weekly meetings will be presentations given by students with internship experience to offer insight into the industry. We will also focus on things like: on what questions to expect in an interview, what to put in your resume, student led mock interviews.
