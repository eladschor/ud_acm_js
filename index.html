Our Mission.


We're an integrated group of students with a passion for technology. Our focus is to provide you with the resources and support to help you be the best developer you can be. We hold weekly meetings, host technical workshops, provide technical career development support, and hack-a-thons throughout the semetser. Bookmark our site to keep tabs on the latest news & events!
